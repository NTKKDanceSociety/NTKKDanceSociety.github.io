About the Academy

Nateshwar Kala Kendra is a premier institution dedicated to the ancient and divine art of Bharatanatyam. Established in 1994 by the esteemed Guru Pallavi Mehendale, the academy has been a nurturing ground for students to immerse themselves in rhythm, expression, and discipline.

Rooted in the classical traditions of Bharatanatyam, the Kendra blends structured technique with spiritual depth. Each class is designed not only to train students in the intricate grammar of the dance form, but to awaken a deeper cultural and emotional understanding of movement, mythology, and devotion.

Our classes are open to students of all age groups and experience levels, guided by the vision of instilling both artistic excellence and inner growth.

“Dance is not merely a performance — it is prayer in motion.”

“Like Lord Nataraja’s cosmic dance, each movement reflects creation, rhythm, and transcendence.”

Classes Offered

Beginner

Introduction to Bharatanatyam, basic adavus, and rhythm.

Intermediate

Varnams, jatiswarams, and body coordination with expressions.

Advanced

Arangetram preparation, choreography, and performance training.
